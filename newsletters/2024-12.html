Project Updates

MB-Demographics

The MB-Demographics "Tools of the Trade" paper has been published! This paper includes lots of practical guidance for authors, reviewers, and editors looking to implement the reporting guidelines outlined in the first MB-Demographics publication. View the paper here.

MB2: Theory of Mind

Tobias Schuwerk (Munich) and Hannes Rakoczy (Göttingen) received funding from the German Research Foundation (DFG) for the second funding period of MB2 studies. The grant supports a three-year PhD position and additional project expenses.

MB4I: Individual differences spin-off

A summary report of our preliminary findings was submitted to KindFund, which supported our study on individual differences in infants’ social preferences and prosocial behaviors across cultures.

MB5: Hunter & Ames model

We are making some final preparations to begin pilot testing in the new year. A project update was recently sent to MB5 listserv. If you didn’t receive it, you can subscribe via this link or by emailing us.
