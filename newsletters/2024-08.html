MB5 Funding Opportunity!


We’re at a point in planning ManyBabies 5 where we need to spend some grant money. We have 15 grants of 2,500 USD to give out over the next few months.

If you want to collect data for MB5 but can’t unless you get some funding, please fill out this form by September 1! On the form, you can justify your need however you wish.

Project Updates

MB2P: Pupil dilation

The MB2P spin-off project has been preregistered on OSF and will move on to data analysis within sub-task groups. Please ensure that you have added yourself to specific tasks in the agenda document.

MB3L: Longitudinal language

The MB3L spin-off project is preparing for data collection! Did your lab collect data for MB3? Participation in MB3L simply involves a CDI data collection 1-1.5 years later! Interested? Any questions? Have a look at the preregistration, the lab manual, and let us know by emailing Anna Exner!
