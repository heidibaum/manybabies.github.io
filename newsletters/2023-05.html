What is ManyBabies?

Join an MB committee

We are looking for individuals to join our committees! This is a great way to contribute to the overarching goals of ManyBabies and network with MB colleagues via a minimal time commitment
(~2 hours/month).  

 

Teaching Training, & Open Science  

Meets monthly to develop teaching and training materials for ManyBabies and the broader developmental science community. Recent efforts include the ManyBabies Workshop Series, as well the production of the “What is ManyBabies?” intro video. 


Diversity & Outreach

Meets monthly to consider ways to promote respectful inclusion, diversity, and outreach in ManyBabies projects. Recent efforts have focused on creating guidelines for inclusive zoom meetings and developing a survey that will enable developmental scientists from around the world to learn about one another’s research.

MB3: Rule Learning

We are currently working on our analysis pipeline. If you enjoy working with Github, R, and related tools, please contact Ingmar Visser or Cátia Oliveira to join.

The follow-up studies have begun planning (MB3L) and data collection (MB3T). Please contact Anna Exner if you would like to join either effort. 

MB4: Social Evaluation

As of this morning, 43 labs are collecting data (with 5 finished!) from 19 countries (Australia, Austria, Canada, China, Colombia, France, Germany, Hong Kong, Israel, Italy, Japan, Korea, Netherlands, New Zealand, Poland, Serbia, Switzerland, US). We extended our data collection deadline to the end of October 2023. We are still recruiting new labs to join us in data collection this summer; please help us spread the word by sharing our sign up information.

MB5: Hunter & Ames model

We are happy to report that we have received very positive reviews on our Stage 1 Registered Report from Nature Human Behaviour. The leadership team is currently taking a first pass through the reviews, and we will be sharing them with the community for input soon. Updates on stimulus development and other next steps were recently shared via the project listserv – make sure you are subscribed to stay up to date!

MB-Demographics

The revised manuscript has been resubmitted to Developmental Psychology. Thanks to all team members for your hard work!

MB-ManyWebcams

We received overall positive feedback from reviewers at Infancy and were invited to submit a major revision. We are currently working on the manuscript and response letter. Contributors: the deadline to contribute to the manuscript revision is June 5.

ManyManys
