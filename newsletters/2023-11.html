Project Updates

MB1N: Native Languages

Data collection continues, and Thierry Nazzi (Paris Descartes University) and his team joined the data collection for IDS preference in French. Welcome to the team!

MB2: Theory of Mind

Data collection is complete! The analysis team is about to finish eye-tracking data preprocessing and we hope to have some first results soon.

MB3: Rule Learning

MB3P Spin-off meeting
The initial meeting of the MB3P (pupil dilation) spin-off is planned for January 16, 16:00-17:00 CET. We will discuss conceptualization, research question, and project planning. (add to calendar)

MB4: Social Evaluation

Data collection is complete! Thank you to all the labs who contributed to data collection. The data analysis team is currently working to analyze the data. Updates will continue to be shared on the MB4 listserv.

MB5: Hunter & Ames model of Infant Attention

Our revised Registered Report is still under review at Nature Human Behaviour. However, we are currently forming teams to work on study documentation (e.g., open materials, including a manual for participating labs) and experimental programs.

If you are interested in joining the Open Documentation Team please email Jessica and, if you are interested in joining the Experiment Programming Team, please email Martin. No hard commitment at this point - practically speaking, emailing us would ensure that you would be invited to the first working group meetings.
