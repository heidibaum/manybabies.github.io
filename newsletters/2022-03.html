announcements
<-- You’ve probably seen this icon next to some author names on journal articles. This is an ORCiD link! An ORCiD is basically a DOI for a researcher that will stick with you through affiliation and name changes. As part of our mission to promote Open Science practices, we strongly recommend that all MB contributors obtain an ORCiD. The sign-up process is fast and easy! If you have any questions/concerns, please email us and we'll be happy to help.

Lancaster Conference on Infant and Early Child Development

LCICD invites abstract submissions relating to any prenatal to early childhood (up to 4 y.o.) aspect of development: perceptual, cognitive, early language and social development. (submit by April 22)

SRCD SECC Dissertation Award

Grad Students: Apply for a $2,000 USD SRCD Student and Early Career Council (SECC) Dissertation Research Funding Award. From the call for applications: “International members and members from underrepresented groups are strongly encouraged to apply.” (apply by May 1)

Institute for Research on Innovation & Science

Online workshop: Joining the Data Revolution: Big Data in Education and Social Science Research; June 6-17, 2022. This is an introductory-level course for researchers in education and social science fields interested in gaining experience working with large-scale restricted-use data (apply by April 1)
