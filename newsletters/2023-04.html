MB5: Hunter & Ames model

Our Registered Report is still under review at Nature Human Behaviour. We received an update letting us know that one reviewer had to drop out, and they were looking for an alternative. In the meantime, the stimulus creation team has been working hard to generate sets of Fribbles and Fractals for use in the study. We plan to send a more thorough update to all contributors soon about stimuli and next steps.
